COCKTAILS

A cocktail is an alcoholic mixed drink, which is either a combination of spirits, or one or more spirits mixed with other ingredients such as fruit juice, flavored syrup, or cream. There are various types of cocktails, based on the number and kind of ingredients added.

4 Classic Drinks To Order at Bar


blackberry-margarita

These margaritas are flavored with a blackberry simple syrup, fresh lime juice, tequila, and orange liqueur. They are a little sweet, a little tart, and just strong enough to make you feel like you're drinking a real margarita (not those syrupy concoctions you get at a restaurant).

Shell Beach

The Shell Beach Cocktail is the perfect beach vacation cocktail recipe made with sparkling Rose' combining homemade Pineapple Ginger Rum and Rose sparkling wine, and orange bitters. This exotic simple but complex combination of flavors is perfect to sip on seas side as you bask in the summer sun.
